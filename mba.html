MBA Events

Public Relations

PR is a strategic communication process that builds mutually beneficial relationships between organisations and their public. As PRO the participants will go through some of the most gruelling yet interesting rounds. The participants will aim to win over the judges through diplomacy, perseverance, tact and communication. PR will provide the perfect environment for the participants to enhance their crisis management skills while taking part in creative yet fun and realistic tasks.

Operations

The "Operations" vertical events revolves around using operation concepts like supply chain management, scheduling notations, lean operation systems, chronological representation, and more to solve complex business problems that come up in the modal production cycle for any company. It will be an interesting and kindling environment which will demand strong problem-solving skills, high integrity and quick thinking.

Marketing

Marketing is what liberates your soul and places your creativity on the biggest platform. Your impression and your ideas shall be etched in every mind. If you are scrambling to put up last minute presentations and yet have strong reason to preserve yours. If you believe you have the ability to go from 0-100 and still wish to pierce higher, if you think out of the box. Marketing is the place for you.

Business Plan

Business Plan is an event that will challenge you to exude creativity, master financials, and execute deliverables flawlessly. If delivering elevator pitches to successful investors and building unicorns has been a dream for you, then look no further! An event that will challenge you on all business forefronts!

Business Quiz

Business quiz is all about the thirst of unquenchable knowledge. It is a fun and educative way to test your knowledge about the nuances of business. You will be challenged with critical questions which will hone your quizzing skills and shape your minds with logical analysis and carefully curated reasoning.

Human Resource

The Human-Resource Vertical is conducted to recognize the most effective team which exhibits the most modern HR qualities. The event is designed in such a manner that all of the qualities that an HR manager should possess in the modern day is tested to its core. The rounds revolve around HR practices like recruitment, selection, HR budgeting, HR mock press etc. Put on your thinking caps and bring out your human side! It's time to engage!

Finance

This event will give you several possibilities to expand your financial knowledge and discover the breadth of finance beyond the limitations of classroom instruction. The “Finance” vertical aims to bring out the contestants' best financial and reasoning skills. The event will help the contestants to bring out their out-of-the-box thinking, aptitude for financial reporting, analytical know-how, technical expertise. Controlling the inflow, outflow, and distribution of money is a key component of financial management since it ensures that everyone has access to enough money to meet their essential needs.

Business Analytics

The Business Analytics event was created to provide an in-house experience of Hackathons and contests presented at Inter-college management fests. The event will test the analytical thinking, problem-solving and decision-making skills and domain knowledge of the contestants. The rounds will be designed in a manner that will help to bring out the skills that modern day business analysts require.

CSR- Corporate Social Responsibility

“Giving back to society is an obligation, not a choice”. Corporate Social responsibility is about running your business while keeping and enhancement of local, regional and global regions. The rounds will be designed to help bring out your skills, team management, out-of-the-box thinking, and stress management provides people with the chance to go beyond the environment and embrace innovation and modelling to create a more sustainable future.

Best Management Team

The "Best Management Team “vertical , will aim at evaluating the team spirit and group cohesion of individual teams along with the managerial skills and abilities possessed by each contestant. The team will consist of 3 members. The rounds will be designed in a manner that will aid the contestants in showcasing their talents and skills of strategic thinking, spontaneous decision making, team work and essential expertise of a manager in the dynamic business environment. Participants will have to approach every round as a team, and eliminations will take place after one to two rounds depending upon the number of registrations.

Best Manager

The “Best Manager” event will be aimed primarily at testing and analyzing the managerial skills possessed by the contestants. The rounds will be designed in a manner that will help to bring out the spontaneity, intuition, out-of-the-box thinking, stress management, cunning and deep analytical skills that modern day managers should possess. The event will span over a period of 2 days, happening on multiple venues across the Kengeri campus. Participants will have to approach every round as an individual unit, and eliminations will take place after every round, leaving one individual left to be crowned as the BEST MANAGER for MAGNOVITE-23.
