Design Events

Revival Outlook

The theme of the event is "Timeless architecture," and participants will select any civilization (notable for its building) from any age to design costumes, jewelry, accessories, and other items that are influenced by the architecture. It will be followed by a ramp walk to present their design inspired by the unique traditions of the civilization. The design/style should not mimic the fashion of the people from the selected time period.

Glimpse into the unfocused

An online photography competition where participants will be given a theme to use and take pictures that are appropriate for entry.

TerrAbstract

A theme will be provided for the participants on spot and they would have to mould an abstract sculpture accordingly.

Design Charrette

Design charrette is a competition for architectural design that aims to generate innovative design concepts. To achieve pleasing and efficient results, you can use any material or technology. To avoid limiting your imagination, the project aims to be open and unrestricted.

Spill Art

Creative art has to be made out of the provided spilled inked sheets all the participants.

Scrape-In

A theme will be provided for the participants and they have to carve out in any one of the medium provided (Pencil/Chalk/Wax/Clay) depending on their creativity (can use online resources) within the time duration.

Digi-Tale

This is a digital event in which the participants will be given a picture which they have to edit keeping in mind the given topic at the time of event. Any design software can be used by the participants. Examples: Photoshop, Illustrator, Inkscape, procreate etc.
Note: Use of artificial intelligence (AI) is not allowed.
