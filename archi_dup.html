Revival Outlook
Rules
x
Rules and Regulations

The theme of the event is Reviving civilizations, and participants will select any civilization (notable for its building) from any age to design costumes, jewellery, accessories, and other items that are influenced by the architecture. It will be followed by a ramp walk to present their design inspired by the unique traditions of the civilization. The design/style should not mimic the fashion of the people from the selected time period.

  1. Time duration: 4 hours
  2. There can be a minimum of 4 Members and a maximum of 8 members on a team.
  3. Each team should have a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 5 members to model the attire.
  4. The event theme is “Reviving civilizations”. The participants will have to choose any particular civilization (prominent in its architecture) be it of any time period and create outfits, jewellery, accessories etc. inspired by the architecture (texture, pattern, colour palette, design) and not the actual clothing of the people of that era.
  5. The participants should plan beforehand and bring the necessary materials and outfits.
  6. Ready-to-wear clothes will not be accepted.
  7. The use of recyclable and sustainable materials will be given preference.
  8. In any way, the designed clothes should not be revealing.
  9. If choosing sleeveless or above-knee-length outfits, the performers must wear a complete body covering underneath.
  10. The judgement of the jury panel will be final.
  11. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  12. The event will take place on campus.
  13. Registration Fee: 750 INR
  14. POCs: Ann Maria Sagi - 8447928904, Yadama Kavya Sri - 9390790686
