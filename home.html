Magnovite

Magnovite (Kengeri Campus)

3rd and 4thMarch, 2023

MAGNOVITE is the flagship annual fest organized by CHRIST (Deemed To Be University), Kengeri Campus. It returns this year with its 12th edition, bringing back its unfading charm.

It has been a legacy of the Kengeri campus, integrating 6 deanery fests into one mega-fest filled with anticipation and zeal. Our aim this year is to make it bigger than ever with an expected footfall of over 4600+ students on campus and over 45+ events.
