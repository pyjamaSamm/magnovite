General Rules and Regulations

Magnovite will be conducted offline at CHRIST (Deemed-to-be University), Kengeri Campus on March 2 & 3, 2023. The fest is open to Undergraduate & Postgraduate students of all disciplines. The fest is a national inter-collegiate competition wherein no CHRIST (Deemed-to-be University) will be participating. The participants should be dressed in complete western formal attire.

All participants are required to carry their respective College ID cards and Valid ID Proof. Participants have to report to the assigned venue at least 30 minutes prior to the start of the event. If you have registered for more than one event, please make sure the event timings do not overlap. Multiple teams from the same university are allowed to participate in the fest.

Unethical practices such as plagiarism, vulgarity, obscenity and violence of any kind will lead to immediate disqualification. Please do not carry hazardous and dangerous articles to the venue. Attempting to use or sell narcotics, drugs, cigarettes, e-cigarettes, alcohol, nicotine delivery devices(such as “vapes”, etc.), or addictive items of any sort is prohibited. Participants must follow all COVID-19 protocols and campus regulations strictly. Registrations will open on (date). The last date to register is (date).

Online registrations in accordance with the above-mentioned deadlines can be done via the registration link provided in the mail or at magnovite.christuniversity.in. Registration fees must only be paid through the South Indian Bank Portal link available on the registration form. The participants can register individually or as a contingent. The registration fee will not be refunded. The Overall Championship will be awarded to the college with the most points at the end of the fest. The points will be awarded according to the following criteria:

1. PARTICIPATION:

Flagship Events:
3 points per team for individual events 5 points per team for duet/group events

Other Events:
1 point per team for individual events 3 points per team for duet/group events

2. WINNERS:

Flagship Events:
25 points per team for First Position 20 points per team for Second Position

Other Events:
20 points per team for First Position 15 points per team for Second Position

Note: The decision of the judges and the organising committee will be final and binding.


  • Note for Outstation Students


    • Participants from Outstation Universities/Colleges need to make their own travel arrangements to participate in Magnovite 2023.

    • Magnovite 2023 encourages the participation of students from outstation universities. Please note that accommodation will be provided to outstation participants at the following cost exclusive of registration fees:

    i. Inside Campus: INR 400 per person, per day
    ii. Outside Campus: INR 700 per person, per day

    • Confirmation of accommodation shall be provided to all the registered outstation students who have completed all the formalities, including payment of the registration fee at least 48 hours prior to the commencement of the Fest.

    • Separate accommodation will be provided for Ladies and Gents.

    • Please note that food will not be provided with the accommodation.

    • A college bus will be provided for transportation to and from the place of stay to the venue at the scheduled bus timings.
